The Softie Structure

The Softie initiative is designed with simplicity and transparency in mind. It is all about making a financial commitment and watching your income grow effortlessly while you mine and accumulate Softcoins.

There are six levels of commitment within Softie, and your level is determined by the amount you commited, which directly influences the percentage of daily returns you earn.

Softie Levels

Level 1 - Rising Softie: The entry-level tier for users with commitments between $5 and $30. With a daily return of 3%, users can earn up to $1 per day. It’s an excellent starting point to test the waters and eventually level up for bigger returns.

Level 2 - Junior Softie: For users who commit between $31 and $100. This tier offers a 4% daily return, potentially yielding up to $4 per day.

Level 3 - Pro Softie: This level caters to users with commitments between $101 and $500. It comes with a 5% daily return, translating to earnings of up to $25 per day.

Level 4 - Expert Softie: Users in this tier have committed between $501 and $2,000 and enjoy a 6% daily return. At this level, users can earn up to $120 per day.

Level 5 - Master Softie: The second-highest level for users who have committed between $2,001 and $5,000. This level offers a 7% daily return, which can amount to up to $350 per day.

Level 6 - Legend Softie: The highest tier, reserved for those who commit more than $5,000. Legend Softies benefit from an 8% daily return, potentially earning 250% of their commitment within a month. The daily earning potential for users at this level is unlimited.

The Softie initiative is structured to accommodate users at all financial levels, ensuring that anyone, regardless of their commitment, has an opportunity to participate and earn.

The Vision

Our vision for Softcoin is to create a lasting and valuable cryptocurrency asset. Building a successful DAO requires robust blockchain infrastructure, which takes time and significant resources to develop. This is why our Token Generation Event is set for one year after the project’s inception.

While building a DAO takes more time and resources, it is the most reliable path to creating a long-lasting and authoritative cryptocurrency. This means that dedicated community members will ultimately realize more profit from Softcoin than from typical memecoins or GameFi projects.
